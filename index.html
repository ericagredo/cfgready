CFG and International Documents Assistance

A Certificate to Foreign Government (CFG) is required by foreign countries in order to sell your products in their territories. A CFG is issued for legally marketed devices in the United States that are in compliance with the requirements of the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) containing information about a product’s regulatory or marketing status. We have over eight years experience in the medical device industry. We will obtain your CFG from the FDA and complete all the required legalization of the documents that you need for countries like Vietnam, Jordan, Costa Rica, Colombia, etc. In 2020 all medical devices will be required to have the Unique Device Identification (UDI). This process could be confusing, time consuming, and frustrating since very few information has been released by the FDA. With our experience in Medical Devices we can assist your organization to obtain the GITIN numbers, correctly labeling and packaging, and submitting to FDA’s GUDID database.
